About Me

Hi! I am now a senior undergraduate student at Department of Electronic Engineering, Tsinghua University. I am working in Institute of Circuit and System since 2016, under the supervision of Prof. Fei Qiao.

I am now a research assistant in Integrated Vision, Intelligent Perception (IVIP) Lab, Tsinghua University, advised by Prof. Fei Qiao and Prof. Qi Wei since 2016. In Summer 2018, I was a visiting student and research assistant at Computational Evolutionary Intelligence (CEI) Lab, advised by Prof. Yiran Chen and Prof. Hai (Helen) Li.

My research interests span from circuit design to architecture design, including hardware-algorithm co-design, low-power VLSI system design, in-memory computing and deep learning.

News: I will join Michigan Intergrated Circuits Laboratory (MICL) this fall to begin my PhD study! My advisors will be Prof. Dennis Sylvester and Prof. David Blaauw.
