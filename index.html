About Me

Hi! I am now a 1st Ph.D. pre-candidate at Department of Electrical & Computer Engineering, University of Michigan. I am working in Michigan Intergrated Circuits Laboratory (MICL) since Aug. 2019, under the supervision of Professor Dennis Sylvester and Professor David Blaauw.

I was a research assistant in Integrated Vision, Intelligent Perception (IVIP) Lab, Tsinghua University, advised by Prof. Fei Qiao and Prof. Qi Wei since 2016. In Summer 2018, I was a visiting student and research assistant at Computational Evolutionary Intelligence (CEI) Lab, advised by Prof. Yiran Chen and Prof. Hai (Helen) Li.

My research interests span from circuit design to architecture design, including hardware-algorithm co-design, low-power VLSI system design, in-memory computing and deep learning.

News: [Sept. 2019] Paper about low power SIFT accelerator has been published in IEEE Transactions on Very Lage Scale Integation Systems.[pdf]
